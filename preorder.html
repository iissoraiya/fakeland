HydraSafe Pre-Order

HydraSafe was developed as a portable, real-time water quality monitoring system integrated into a reusable bottle. The system utilizes pH and TDS sensors, which measure the acidity/alkalinity and the concentration of dissolved ions in water, respectively. These sensors are connected to a microcontroller, which processes sensor data and compares it to predefined safety thresholds. The results are displayed on an LCD screen and translated into user-friendly outputs through a color-coded LED system and a buzzer alert, allowing immediate interpretation of water conditions ranging from critical to good.
